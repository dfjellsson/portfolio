Projects

Boost (App)

Introducing the app BOOST: "One chat at a time". A Figma prototype for a unique community app where women can engage in private conversations, discussing daily dilemmas with empathy and support. Designed to create a safe and welcoming space, BOOST is supposed to empower women to connect on a deeper level, sharing their experiences and seeking advice from those who understand.

Kino Koszyk (Webpage)

This is a project that I made together with a few other designers and web devolopers for the independent company Kino Koszyk. Our design showcases their creative documentaries, books, and exhibitions. Adapting across devices, the site offers user-friendly navigation and multimedia integration, providing a window into Kino Koszyk's library of content.
